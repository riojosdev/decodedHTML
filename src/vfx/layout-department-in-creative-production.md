# Layout Department in Creative Production

## What is the Layout Stage?
Involves places virtual cameras, characters and objects within the 3D environment to establish the composition, framing and spatial relationships.

## What is Look Development?
Pre-production phase, where a show or movie's overall realistic, artistic or stylized appearance are established. Involves creating and adjusting materials, lighting, shdows , textures and other visual properties of 3D elements to achieve the desired visual rendering.

## Who is a Layout Artist? What do they do?
Determines camera angles, movements and overall scene composition
