# Color Correction & Color Grading

## What is Color Correction?
Color correction, normalizes the footage closer to how our eyes actually see. When you take a footage, your take may contain irregularites. Either due to the environment or do to hardware limits or configurations. Color correcting is a process that fixes this. Common adjustments done here are, changes to the saturation, contrast.. etc

## What is Color Grading?
Color grading adds stylized coloring techniques to bring the creative production closer to the aesthetics of the story. Bring more emotion and attention towards the story.
